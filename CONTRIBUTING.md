# Contributing to Lenga Engine

Thank you for your interest in Lenga Engine.

At this stage of development, contributions are **intentionally restricted** while the engine’s core architecture is being finalized.

---

## Current Contribution Policy

**Status:** Closed (Pre-Alpha)

During this phase:

- The public roadmap is visible
- Architectural decisions are still evolving
- APIs are unstable and subject to change

To avoid churn and fragmentation, we are **not accepting pull requests** at this time.

---

## What *Is* Welcome

You are welcome to:

- Open **issues** for:
  - Bugs
  - Crashes
  - Build problems
  - Clear architectural concerns
- Provide **design feedback** or questions
- Follow progress and roadmap updates

---

## What Is Not Accepted (For Now)

- Feature pull requests
- API expansions
- Refactors
- Engine-level optimizations
- Editor tooling contributions

These will open once Milestone 1–3 are complete.

---

## Future Contribution Phases

Planned future phases include:

1. **Runtime Stabilization**
2. **Editor Prototyping**
3. **Public Alpha**
4. **Community Contributions**

Each phase will include:
- Contribution guidelines
- Coding standards
- Review process
- ADR (Architecture Decision Record) references

---

## Contact

If you are interested in **long-term collaboration** or core engine work, keep an eye on official announcements.

---

*Thank you for respecting the stability of the project during its early stages.*
